THE MATHINATOR ALPHA

Welcome to The Mathinator

The Mathinator is an application that does algebra step-by-step. The goal of the project is to have a platform/tool that assists learning for students in algebra & calculus.

Currently the project is in alpha state, so it has a few bugs. We wish to have to new features soon, as the the Mathinator can only simplify and solve multi-step equations at this time. The future looks bright, we are currently working on factoring and a step by step derivative calculator.

The Mathinator should be fully equipped by Fall 2012.
