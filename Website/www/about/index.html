Welcome to The Mathinator

The Mathinator is a free online site that assists in the solving of both simple and complex math. This tool was designed, created, and thought up by Patrick Lorio. It aims to help students and young adults with math by solving problems and showing step by step the process in which they were solved. Our company hopes to continue adding and improving on the mathinator. We are contemplating a mobile app for the mathinator, but have not yet begun building it. Please enjoy our intuitive and unique services this coming school year and in the years to come.

The website is currently in Alpha testing so there are a few bugs. If you find any please let us know at themathinator.com@gmail.com.
